Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a flight?

Tap the + button in the top right corner of the Flights tab. Enter your flight number, departure and arrival airports, and date.

Why isn't my flight status updating?

Flight data is refreshed automatically based on your flight's schedule. You can also pull down on the flight list to refresh manually. Make sure you have an active internet connection.

How do I sync flights across my devices?

Sign in with the same account on all your devices. Your flights will sync automatically via the cloud.

How do I enable Live Activities?

Go to Settings → Live Activities and toggle it on. Your active flights will appear on your Lock Screen and Dynamic Island automatically.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings → AeroStats Premium → Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the Settings app → Apple ID → Subscriptions → AeroStats → Cancel Subscription.

How many devices can I use?

Free accounts support 1 device. Premium subscribers can use up to 3 devices simultaneously.
