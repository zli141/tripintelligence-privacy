Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Overview

AeroStats ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • Account information – email address used to sign in
  • Flight data – flight numbers, routes, and dates you enter
  • Device identifiers – used to manage multi-device sync and enforce device limits
  • Advertising identifier (IDFA) – collected only with your explicit permission, used to show relevant ads
  • Push notification token – used to deliver flight status alerts

How We Use Your Information

  • To sync your flights across your devices via Firebase
  • To send flight status notifications (gate changes, delays, boarding)
  • To display Live Activities on your Lock Screen
  • To show advertisements and keep the app free
  • To enforce subscription and device access limits

Third-Party Services

AeroStats uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google) – authentication, cloud storage, and push notifications
  • Google AdMob – advertising
  • FlightAware – live flight tracking data
  • AeroDataBox – flight status and schedule data
  • OpenSky Network – live aircraft position data

Advertising and Tracking

We use Google AdMob to show ads. When you grant App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission, your advertising identifier may be used to show personalized ads. You can opt out at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, or within the app's Settings.

If you subscribe to AeroStats Premium, ads are removed entirely.

Data Retention

Your flight data and account information are stored until you delete your account. You can delete your account and all associated data from within the app at any time.

Children's Privacy

AeroStats is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
